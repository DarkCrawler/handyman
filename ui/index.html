HandyMan app V0.1

What is it about ?

HandyMan acts as an infrastructure to on demand service app. Currently aiming to digitalize the data for any upcoming startup with vendor-client data. You can define the models that your app would be requiring, whip up the web services that you would require to connect your user interface with your backend models and get going to collect your user/service data for future use

Whats in v0.1

  • Add Customer & Vendor
  • Add Orders
  • Edit Customer/Vendor/Order
  • Search Customer/Vendor/Order
